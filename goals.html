Galvanize

To Learn How Things Work

The Way Things Work by David Macaulay has been my favorite book since I was a child. I'm very much a visual and hands on learner. In the next 24 weeks my goal is to take the concepts learned in class and expand upon them to fully understand and implement them in ways I hadn’t originally thought of.
