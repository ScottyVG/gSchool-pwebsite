Who is Scott?

Scott Van Gilder is originally from a small town of 3,000 people called Jefferson, Iowa. After completing his degree in Mechanical Engineering, Scott moved to Colorado for work, the mountains and to be closer to his brother who was doing his DMA at the University of Colorado.

Scott's core value is learning. Outside of work, he has a myriad of hobbies that require a ton of research and experimenting. From gardening vegetables on his roof, to bird watching the threatened habitats of the prairie and peregrine falcons in Boulder's open space & mountain parks, to name a few.

Mr Kitty

Mr. Kitty was adopted into our home after the flood of 2013. His owners thought he didn't like people and abandoned him when they moved out of their house. Mr. Kitty lived on the street for about 2 months before my neighbor who was caring for him asked if we would take him in.

He now has a wonderful home and enjoys taking long naps, eating lots of cat nip and being the neighborhood bruiser.

♥Wedding Bells in the Future♥

I met my fiancé Brightin in the summer of 2011 at the World Famous Dark Horse. I was invited to her birthday party through a mutual friend and it was love at first sight. Everyday is a new and exciting adventure together. We enjoy cooking, hiking, trail running, biking and making music together. Brightin is a well-established professional Violin & Viola player in the Colorado region and I’m always delighted to hear her play.

Our wedding is planned for October 7th, 2016 at the St. Malo Chapel near Allenspark.
